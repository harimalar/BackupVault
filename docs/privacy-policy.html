Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

BackupVault is a local-first macOS backup app. We designed it to work without accounts, cloud syncing, analytics, or advertising.

What BackupVault accesses

  • Folders and drives you explicitly choose for backup or restore.
  • Optional NAS or SMB locations you explicitly configure.
  • Basic app settings stored on your Mac, such as schedule and appearance preferences.

What BackupVault does not collect

  • No account information.
  • No cloud-stored backup content.
  • No analytics events.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party tracking data.

Where your data is stored

Your backups are stored only on the destination you select, such as an external drive or NAS share. The app does not upload your files to a remote service operated by us.

NAS credentials

If you connect to an SMB or NAS destination, macOS may prompt you for credentials. Those credentials are handled by macOS and may be stored in Keychain if you choose to save them.
